About Doctoray:

1. What is Doctoray?

Doctoray is an online service, where you can book an appointment or immediately contact a healthcare professional via chat, phone call or video call.

2. How does Doctoray work?

Doctoray is easy to use. All you need is a laptop, PC with web cam, iPhone, or an iPad. You choose the time and date when you see the doctor of your choice. You pay online using our safe payment gateway and then start contacting the doctor via chat, phone call or video call. For more information please have a look at our How it works page.

3. Can you actually see the doctor?

Yes, you may see the doctor face to face through a secure video call, where you can discuss you medical condition with him/her privately in you own place. Please have a look at our How it works page for more explanation.

About using Doctoray:

1. How can I get the app?

If you are an Apple device user, you can simply search Doctoray in the app store or click the link to the app store in our website home page. Unfortunately we don’t have an app for the android for the time being but we are working on it and it will be available soon. However you can access the website using your internet browser using your android or windows device.

2. Can Doctoray deal with medical emergencies?

In case of an emergency you should NOT use Doctoray. If you think you have an emergency symptom or a person you are assisting has an emergency condition you should immediately call 112 or take the patient to the accident and emergency department in a governmental hospital. Example of emergency conditions are: difficulty breathing, sever bleeding, chest pain, loss of consciousness, acute confusional state, and seizures which are not stopping, if you or someone else believes that a person is having a heart attack or a stroke.

3. How old do I have to be to use Doctoray?

You have to be 18 years old and older, or 13 years and older and have both your parents permission and a permission of a payer. However, if you are 18 years old or older and you want to use Doctoray to consult with your doctor about a child of yours or a relative you can. All you have to do is to tick the box saying parent in the profile page.

4. How can I book an appointment?

To book an appointment please press the “Find a Doctor” button in any page of our website or in the mobile app and it will lead you to the page where you can find your preferred doctor. When you find the doctor you are seeking you just need to click on his/her profile and it will lead you to his/her detail page where you can choose the date and time you want to see the doctor. Please note that you will be only able to choose the appointments that are available.

5. How does the instant consultation work?

Instead of booking an appointment for a specific time and date, you may brows in our list of doctors available instantly, where you can connect with the doctor immediately. In case of a busy doctor you may be given the option of being kept in a queue to wait when your turn to see the doctor is on, in which case you will receive a notification that your session is about to start.

6. Can I cancel the appointment or change the appointment time or date?

It is free to cancel the appointment 48 hours before the appointment session starts. Cancelation less than 48 hours before the session starts will cost 50% of the consultation price. Cancelation is not possible for instant consultations. To change the consultation time or date, you have to cancel the appointment and book another appointment.
