About Us

Doctoray is the best possible way to seek medical help as it connects doctors with people, creating a wonderful experience for both doctors and patients. We provide people and doctors with a simpler, easier healthcare experience.

Doctor Consultations are becoming difficult these days. Our Platform is an endeavor to bring back your doctor to you to provide medical advice to you, whenever or from wherever you want. By providing innovative online consultation solutions, we aim to overcome barriers to access while promoting continuity of care and successful, cost-conscious treatment online.

Doctoray for Patients

Doctoray helps people to seek medical advice whenever and from wherever they want thus, helping people to make informed decision about their health.

Doctoray for Doctors

Doctoray enables Doctors to save their time and increase their practice by interacting with patients from different areas.
