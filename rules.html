Rules And Regulations For Doctors

The Doctor shall in order to avail the services of the Platform abide the following rules and regulations thereto:

1

You shall provide true and correct information at the time of registration and opening an account on the Platform and shall be solely responsible for maintaining the accuracy of the information.

2

You agree to submit your relevant documents when the Company asks for the same to enable the Company to conduct a background check prior to listing you on the Platform.

3

Doctors assigned to a particular patient shall respond promptly to a communication received through the online doctor consultation program.

4

You shall provide the best advice possible using the information available through the online consultation program.

5

In the event you feel that more information is required or a physical examination must be conducted before any medical advice or prescription can be given, then you must state those limitations and requirements to the patient or user promptly.

6

You should apply the usual principles for obtaining patient’s informed consent, protecting patient’s privacy and protecting patient’s rights to confidentially

7

You should reveal your identity known to the patient

8

You should confirm the identity of the patient at each consultation.

9

You should provide an explanation to the patient of the particular process involved in the online consultation

10

You should access the patient’s condition, based on the history and clinical signs and appropriate examination

11

You should ensure that you communicate with the patient to:

a

Establish the patient’s current medical condition and past medical history, and current or recent use of medications, including non-prescription medicationsYou should access the patient’s condition, based on the history and clinical signs and appropriate examination

b

Identify the likely cause of the patient’s condition

c

Ensure that there is sufficient clinical justification for the proposed treatment

d

Ensure that the proposed treatment is not contra-indicated.

12

You shall be solely responsible for evaluating information used in assessment and treatment, irrespective of its source. This applies to information gathered by a third party who may have taken a history from, or examined, the patient

13

You should make appropriate arrangements to follow the progress of the patient.

14

You should at all times keep an appropriate record of the consultation provided by you.

15

The online consultation services provided through our Platform shall not be regarded as a substitute of medical services provided at hospital and you should not hesitate to ask the patient to visit the hospital whenever needed.
